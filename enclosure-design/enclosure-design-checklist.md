# Enclosure Design Review Checklist

This checklist summarizes practical review points for mechanical enclosure design in product development.

## 1. Basic Structure

- Check enclosure size and available internal space
- Check wall thickness
- Check rib structure
- Check boss structure
- Check cover and base alignment
- Check assembly direction
- Check internal component clearance

## 2. Fastening Structure

- Check screw size and quantity
- Check boss strength
- Check thread engagement length
- Check risk of boss cracking
- Check washer or insert requirements
- Check tightening accessibility
- Check torque control requirement

## 3. Cover and Deformation Review

- Check cover flatness
- Check deformation during screw tightening
- Check center lifting or bending
- Check contact with internal switches or components
- Check tolerance stack-up between cover and internal parts
- Check need for additional support ribs or stopper features

## 4. Sealing Surface

- Check sealing surface flatness
- Check gasket or O-ring contact area
- Check sealing compression
- Check surface roughness requirement
- Check risk of uneven compression
- Check foreign material or burr risk

## 5. Assembly Review

- Check assembly sequence
- Check tool access
- Check risk of incorrect assembly
- Check interference between parts
- Check cable routing space
- Check maintenance accessibility

## 6. Manufacturability

- Check machining feasibility
- Check injection molding or die-casting feasibility
- Check draft angle
- Check undercut risk
- Check tolerance capability
- Check burr removal requirement
- Check cost and process variation

## 7. Failure Review Points

- Cover deformation
- Boss cracking
- Screw loosening
- Sealing failure
- Internal interference
- Assembly variation
- Dimensional mismatch
- Early-life field issue

## Notes

This document is a generalized engineering checklist.  
It does not include confidential drawings, customer information, internal test reports, or proprietary project data.
